federal aid was approved friday for tornado-ravaged huntsville, al where 17 died, more than 400 were injured, 80 in hospital care, and about 1,000 left homeless.
119 houses were destroyed, 125 had minor damage, and three churches, two schools, 35 businesses and 10 public buildings were either destroyed or suffered heavy damage.
tornado winds were estimated at 250 mph. state officials estimated the damage in excess of $100 million.
about $1 million may be needed for clothing and temporary housing.
the federal government pays 75% and the state 25%.
federal checks are expected to be on the way within the week.

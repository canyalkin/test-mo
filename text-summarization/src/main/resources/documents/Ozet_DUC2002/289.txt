japanese found the foreign practice of using his given name impolite and referred to him simply as his majesty, the emperor.
japanese histories will record him as emperor showa, the name he chose when he became emperor in 1926.
his son, akihito succeeded emperor hirohito and has chosen the name heisei.
japan numbers the years according to the emperor's tenure on the thrown.
thus the first year of hirohito's reign was showa 1.

memorial services were held on saturday for the five school children slain by a suicidal drifter.
patricia busher, principal of cleveland elementary school, where the killings occurred, called it a senseless tragedy that could have happened anywhere.
she spoke at the central methodist church where services were held for the four cambodian children who were killed.
a separate catholic mass and burial were held earlier for the vietnamese girl who was killed.

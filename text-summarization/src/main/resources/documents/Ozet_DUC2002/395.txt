the battle for tiananmen square was fought primarily in the streets leading to the square where scores of people fell trying to prevent troops and armored military vehicles from moving on the students camped in the square.
the first troops entered the square about 1 a.m. sunday followed by a huge convoy of armored vehicles that surrounded the few thousand students that remained.
a propaganda battle by loudspeaker ensued.
by 4:30 a.m. the students decided by voice vote to leave the square to avoid further bloodshed.
as dawn broke rifle-toting troops ruled the vast expanse occupied the day before by students seeking a freer society.

one of the three owners of cory, a champion german short-haired pointer is suing the other two for damages and to determine if cory is officially retired.
cory has won 245 shows as best-of-breed and 19 awards as best-in-show.
plaintiff attalla claims the owners' agreement specifies that he advances fees to show the dog and he decides when cory retires.
he is suing the other two owners, john and rita remondi for $125,000 in fees and to stop them from showing cory, who has been officially retired as announced in dog news.
the remondis claim that cory's retirement is fraudulent.
